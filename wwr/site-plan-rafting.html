Overview

Welcome to our rafting site plan! Here, we outline the key elements for establishing an online presence for our rafting business. From defining our purpose to outlining our branding and style guide, this plan serves as a roadmap for our digital journey.

Purpose:

The purpose of establishing an online presence for the rafting business is to expand brand visibility, reach a broader audience of adventure enthusiasts, increase bookings, and provide convenient access to information about the rafting services offered.

Audience

Who is the target audience for our water rafting website?

  1. Scenario Question: Are you an adventure enthusiast looking for thrilling outdoor experiences?

    Answer: Our website caters to adventure enthusiasts seeking exciting water rafting adventures.

  2. Scenario Question: Are you planning a group outing or team-building event?

    Answer: Our website provides information and packages tailored to group outings and team-building activities.

  3. Scenario Question: Are you a first-time rafter seeking guidance and information about rafting safety?

    Answer: Our website offers comprehensive resources and safety guidelines for first-time rafters.
